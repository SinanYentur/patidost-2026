# Privacy Policy - Patidost Enterprise (2026 Ready)
## 1. DATA COLLECTION
- **Auth Data:** Email and UID are collected for account management.
- **Social Content:** User posts and media are stored securely via Firebase.

## 2. DATA RIGHTS (GDPR Art. 17 & 20)
- **Deletion:** Users can delete their account via in-app settings.
- **Portability:** Data export is available in SPDX/JSON format.

## 3. SECURITY
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
