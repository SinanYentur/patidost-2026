# Roadmap & ADR - Patidost Sovereign (2024-2026)
## ARCHITECTURE DECISION RECORDS (ADR)
- **ADR-001 (Cross-Platform):** Use Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) for shared business logic.
- **ADR-002 (Moderation):** Multi-layered AI + Human review pipeline.
- **ADR-003 (Privacy):** Real-time data masking and GDPR compliance by design.
- **ADR-004 (Real-time):** WebSockets for Chat and FCM for Notifications.
- **ADR-005 (Safety):** Integrated Age Verification and Crisis Intervention.

## ROADMAP
- **Q1 2024:** Foundation (Auth, Profiles, Compliance).
- **Q2 2024:** Social Core (Feed, Messaging, Real-time).
- **2026:** Android 15/16 Hardening & NPU Optimization.
